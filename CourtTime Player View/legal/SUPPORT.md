## Frequently asked questions

### Booking and reservations

**How do I book a court?**

Open the **Book** tab, pick a date, choose a court, then select your start and end time. Tap **Confirm Booking**. You'll get a push notification confirmation immediately.

For the soonest open slot, use the **Quick Book** section on the Home tab — it shows the next available 1-hour slots across every bookable court at your facility, with one-tap booking.

**How do I cancel a booking?**

On the Home tab, find the booking under **Upcoming Bookings** and tap **Cancel**. You'll be asked to confirm. Cancellations are allowed up until your reservation start time.

**How do I edit a booking?**

Tap **Edit** next to any upcoming booking. You can change the date, court, start time, or end time.

**A court is showing as "walk-up only" — what does that mean?**

Some facilities reserve specific courts for walk-up play (no online booking). You'll see those courts in a banner on the Book tab — to play on them, just show up at the facility.

**Why does my booking violate facility rules?**

Each facility sets its own rules: how far ahead you can book, daily limits, peak-hour restrictions, household limits, and more. When you try to book outside those rules, the app explains exactly which rule was violated. If you think a rule is wrong or shouldn't apply to you, contact your facility administrator directly.

### Drills, events, and bulletin board

**How do I sign up for a drill?**

Open the **Community** tab and switch to **Bulletin**. Drill posts show the date, court, and how many spots are open. Tap **Sign Up**. If the drill is full, you'll be added to the waitlist and notified if a spot opens.

**Can I cancel my drill signup?**

Yes — open the drill post and tap **Cancel Signup**. If you're confirmed and there's a waitlist, the next person in line will be auto-promoted.

**Why can't I sign up for a drill?**

Some drills are limited to a specific gender (set by the facility). To be eligible, set your gender in **Profile → Edit Profile** so the system knows whether you qualify.

### Hitting partners

**How do I find a hitting partner?**

Open **Community → Partners**. Browse posts from other members, filter by skill level, and tap **Message** to start a conversation. You can also post your own request by tapping **Post Looking for Partner**.

### Account and profile

**How do I change my password?**

Tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen and follow the email link. We'll send a one-time reset link to the email on your account.

**How do I update my profile, skill level, or USTA rating?**

Open **Profile → Edit Profile**. Changes save when you tap **Save**.

**How do I delete my account?**

See our [Account Deletion](/delete-account) page for instructions and a list of what gets deleted vs. retained. Deletion is free and immediate — you do not need to email us first, but you can if you'd prefer we handle it for you.

**I belong to multiple facilities. How do I switch?**

Tap the facility name at the top of any tab (Book, Community, Home, Messages) to open the facility switcher. Your selection persists across tabs.

### Notifications

**How do I turn off push notifications?**

Open **Profile → Notifications** and toggle the master switch off, or disable specific categories (booking updates, reminders, strikes, announcements, weather).

**I'm not getting push notifications.**

Check three things:

1. Push notifications are enabled in your phone's system settings for CourtTime.
2. The **Push notifications** master toggle is on under **Profile → Notifications**.
3. The category for the notification you expected (e.g. **Booking reminders**) is also on.

If everything looks right but notifications still aren't arriving, email us at [reidbissell@courttimeapp.com](mailto:reidbissell@courttimeapp.com) — include your phone model and OS version.

### Strikes and lockouts

**Why did I get a strike?**

Each facility sets its own no-show and late-cancellation policy. Common reasons: not showing up for a reserved court, cancelling within the facility's late-cancellation window, or violating a posted rule.

Strikes are issued by your facility administrator. If you think a strike was issued in error, contact your facility directly — only your facility can revoke a strike.

**My account is locked out — what now?**

Lockouts are temporary and end on the date shown on your Home screen. While locked out, you can still see other members and read announcements but cannot book courts. To restore booking privileges sooner, contact your facility administrator.

### Messaging

**Can I message anyone in CourtTime?**

You can message any active member of the same facility you belong to. Members of other facilities are not visible to you, and inactive (pending, suspended, expired) members are filtered out of the new-message picker.

**Where do my old messages go?**

Conversations are scoped per facility. If you're not seeing a past conversation, switch to the right facility using the dropdown at the top of the Messages tab.

---

### Privacy and data

**Who can see my profile and messages?**

Only active members of the same facility you belong to can see your name, profile photo, skill level, and USTA rating. Your email, phone, and address are never shown to other members. Your messages are visible only to the person you're messaging.

For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

**Do you sell my data?**

No. We never sell personal data and we don't run third-party advertising trackers.
